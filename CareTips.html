DIY Succulent Soil

  1. Ingredients:
    • Perlite or pumice (porous)
    • Sand (drainage)
    • Organic matter (nutrients)
  2. Measurements:
    • One part perlite or pumice
    • Two parts sand
    • Two parts organic matter
  3. Mixing:
    • Mix equal ratios as listed above
    • Wetten the mixture as you mix it together

Arrangement Ideas

Arrangements can be made from many different types of succulents. Using recycled pots and anything that could house soil and roots is perfect to use. Old logs, water cans, wheel barrels, bowls, pots, etc can be used for arrangements. Simple add your succulent soil, plant the succulents you want, and water enough to make the soil wet.
