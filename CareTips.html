DIY Succulent Soil

  1. Ingredients:
    • Perlite or pumice (porous)
    • Sand (drainage)
    • Organic matter (nutrients)
  2. Measurements:
    • One part perlite or pumice
    • Two parts sand
    • Two parts organic matter
  3. Mixing:
    • Mix equal ratios as listed above
    • Wetten the mixture as you mix it together
